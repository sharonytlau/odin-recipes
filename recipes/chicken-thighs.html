Honey-Garlic Slow Cooker Chicken Thighs

Description

Always a hit with adults and kids. Serve with basmati rice or quinoa and steamed or roasted vegetables. A recipe by Myrna from allrecipes.

Ingredients

  • 44 skinless, boneless chicken thighs
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ½ cup ketchup
  • ⅓ cup honey
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil

Steps

  1. Lay chicken thighs into the bottom of a 4-quart slow cooker.
  2. Whisk soy sauce, ketchup, honey, garlic, and basil together in a bowl; pour over the chicken.
  3. Cook on Low for 6 hours.
